1. PERSONA DEFINITION

Tên persona, background story (2-3 câu), 5 personality traits

POV / Ngôi kể: ghi rõ ngôi kể + cách xưng hô

Thang đo: Formal<->Casual, Humor, Expert<->Observer, Speed, Warmth<->Cold (mỗi thang 1-10)

2. VOICE RULES

DO: 5-7 rules, DON'T: 5-7 rules

Sentence structure, paragraph length, rhythm pattern

3. VOICE RULES BLOCK (copy-paste cho Tool 1)

1 block text paste trực tiếp vào system prompt Tool 1

PHẢI bao gồm POV/cách xưng hô rõ ràng

4. BENCHMARK PASSAGES

3 đoạn mẫu (100-150 từ): Hook, Giải thích, Dramatic moment

5. FORMATTING RULES

Script format, emphasis/pause markers, naming convention

6. THUMBNAIL TEXT BANK

HEADING BẮT BUỘC: "## 6. THUMBNAIL TEXT BANK"

20 mẫu text (2-4 từ), chia theo:

"#### Category: Question", "#### Category: Statement",

"#### Category: Number", "#### Category: Emotion"

Sub-sections BẮT BUỘC:

"### Font Recommendation"

"### Color Palette" - bảng có "Background | #hex" + "Accent | #hex"

"### Thumbnail Composition Rules"

7. CHANNEL STRATEGY

Monetization roadmap, content frequency, topics nên tránh

Format: Markdown. Voice Rules Block phải copy-paste ready.
